**HC-05**

  * Default baud: 38400
  * 6Pin (EN pin)
  * 시리얼 터미널은 Both NL-CR 세팅

**HC-06**

  * Default baud: 9600
  * 4Pin
  * Line ending 없음
  * <a href="http://www.micro4you.com/files/ElecFreaks/Bluetooth%20HC-06.pdf" target="_blank">AT command</a>
