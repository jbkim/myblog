**인덕티브 센서 lj12a3-4-z/bx**

Inductive type prox switch with NPN Normally Open transistor switch.  
Output upon detection.  
P/N: LJ12A3-4-Z/BX  
Power: 6-36VDC  
Output Current: 300mA (Sink)  
Detection Range: 4mm  
Detected material: Iron/Steel alloys
