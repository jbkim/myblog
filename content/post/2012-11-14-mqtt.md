  <p >
    <span >MQTT stands for MQ Telemetry Transport. It<br /> is a publish/subscribe, extremely simple and lightweight messaging protocol,<br /> designed for constrained devices and low-bandwidth, high-latency or unreliable<br /> networks. The design principles are to minimise network bandwidth and device<br /> resource requirements whilst also attempting to ensure reliability and some<br /> degree of assurance of delivery. These principles also turn out to make the<br /> protocol ideal of the emerging “machine-to-machine” (M2M) or “Internet of<br /> Things” world of connected devices, and for mobile applications where bandwidth<br /> and battery power are at a premium.</span>
